In nest.js Files are named as follows:
1. DTO: filename.dto.ts
2. Controller: filename.controller.ts
3. Module: filename.module.ts
4. Service/Provider: filename.service.ts
5. InterfaceFile: filename.interface.ts
6. typesFile: filename.d.ts
7. middleware: Filename.middleware.ts
8. pipe: FileName.pipe.ts
Testing files:
 1. For Service: filename.service.spec.ts
 2. For Controller: filename.controller.spec.ts
